Achari Chicken

  1. Home
  2. Asparagus Orzo
  3. Crispy Gnocchi
  1. 2 tbsp canola oil
  2. 2 tsp mustard seeds
  3. 2 tsp fenugreek seeds
  4. 2 tsp cumin
  5. 2 tsp fennel seeds
  6. 1.5 tsp nigella seeds
  7. 181g onion, diced
  8. 4 dried red chiles
  9. 2 tsp salt
  10. 1 tsp turmeric
  11. 4 tsp kashmiri chili
  12. 1 tbsp ginger, finely grated
  13. 1 tbsp garlic, finely grated
  14. 1.75 lbs chicken thigh, cubed
  15. 1/2 cup water
  16. 1.5 tbsp tomato paste
  17. Juice of half lemon

Step 1: Heat canola oil in an instapot on saute mode.

Step 2: Add mustard seed. Once pot starts spitting add fenugreek, cumin, fennel, and nigella and saute for 30 seconds.

Step 3: Add onion and red chillies, sauteing until onions start to become translucent.

Step 4: Add ginger, garlic, turmeric, kashmiri chili, and salt then mix well.

Step 5: Add chicken and stir until coated in spices. Cook a little bit longer until chick has tiny bit of color to it.

Step 6: Turn off saute mode and add 1/2 cup of water to deglaze it. Stir.

Step 7: Add the tomato paste on top of everything. DO NOT STIR IT IN - it will cause it to burn.

Step 8: Pressure cook on high for 8 minutes letting it vent naturally.

Step 9: Once done, open, stir, then add lemon juice.

Step 10: Serve with basmati rice.
