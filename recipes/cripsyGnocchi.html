Crispy Gnocchi

  1. Home
  2. Achari Chicken
  3. Asparagus Orzo
  1. 5 ounces baby spinach
  2. 6 ounces Greek feta cheese, crumbled
  3. 1 lemon, halved
  4. 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  5. Salt and black pepper
  6. 16 ounces potato gnocchi
  7. 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  8. 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  9. Big handful of chopped dill
  10. Big handful of chopped mint

Step 1: Place the baby spinach into a large bowl. Add half of the feta (~85 grams), juice of 1/2 lemon, 1 tablespoon of olive oil, and a large pinch of salt; rub the ingredients vigorously into the spinach to tenderize it. Leave it to sit and soften further while you cook.

Step 2: Heat a large cast-iron or nonstick skillet over medium-high (I use a 5 on my stovetop); add 2 tablespoons of olive oil. Add the gnocchi to the pan, breaking up any that are stuck together. Cook for 8 to 10 minues, tossing every 2 minutes, until golden and crispy. Allow to coll in the pan for 5 minutes.

Step 3: Add the gnocchi to the spinach, along with the red pepper, scallions, dill and mint. You may drizzle an additional 2 tablespoons of olive oil (although I opt out of this), add the remaining crumbled feta (~85 grams) and toss to combine. Squeeze the remaing lemon juice from the other 1/2 lemon.
